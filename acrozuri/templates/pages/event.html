Jams

What is this even? Whenever the weather permits, we love to gather somewhere outside and play together. These so-called jams are self-organized, usually in our Telegram Group. Most jams take place at either Chinawiese or Josefwiese. It’s a great opportunity to get a sense of what Acroyoga is all about and meet some new people. There’s really worse things to do than being out in the sun and having fun.
Find listed below a few of the regular jams in Zürich, keep in mind that on good weather conditions participants might prefere to be outdoors.


Zirkus Quartier Acroyoga Jam

The oldest Acroyoga jam in Züri. Zirkus Quartier has giving shelter to this jam for about 5 years now, come, join and meet the community. If you have any questions regarding experience needed or so we recommend you to visit our FAQ

  • Jam Date: Thursdays
  • Jam Time: 19:15
  • Location: Flurstrasse 85, 8047 Zürich
  • Donation: CHF 5

Open Training Zirkus Quartier

Ever wondered how a Zirkus Open Training looks like ? This is a good opportunity to see many different disciplines on the training floor. For a year or two, the Acro community as been regularly going there to practice Acro in all its variations. Feel free to join! Questions? Check our FAQ

  • Jam Date: Mondays
  • Jam Time: 17:30
  • Location: Flurstrasse 85, 8047 Zürich
  • Donation: CHF 5

Saturday Acro Verein Jam

The one an online Verein hosted jam (for now ;)) we're working hard on making it as hellcoming for everyone as we can! Recently we got new matts and are constantly looking to improve our infrastructure to have a safe and fun place to share acro with everyone!FAQ

  • Jam Date: Saturday
  • Jam Time: 13:30
  • Location: , 8047 Zürich
  • Donation: CHF 5 Members: Free

Other Groups and Jams

Other Acrobatics groups have regular meetings. The Rote Fabrik Group or the Zirkus Robinson have regular jams. ASVZ doesn't exactly have jams but checking out the Manege if definitly worth it. For other questions look our FAQ

  • Jam Date: Tuesday, Thuersday & Sunday
