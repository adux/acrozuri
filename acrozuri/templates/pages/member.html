what we're working on now

As the first board of the verein its important to set a solid fundation. We're developing security standards, getting access to halls,working on all the paper work required and talking with all of you to find a common vision for this community. But not everything is long term, there are many problems that need to be addressed in the short term, in particular we're working on:

  • We need a logo!
  • Finding traning spaces
  • Organizing an flash mob
  • Ensurance for the traning spaces
  • Setting a solid Financial ground
  • Connecting with other associations

If you feel you want to help in any of this tasks, have any cool ideas to share, or have any suggestions just get in touch by clicking HERE and write us.

By becoming a member of the Acro Züri Verein you will help grow the Acrobatics and AcroYoga community in Zurich. Together, we will be able to provide training spaces, invite teachers, organize events and keep prices as low as possible to make Acrobatics and AcroYoga accessible to everyone. We don’t like to think of you becoming a member as being of transactional nature. Rather, we’d like everyone to feel like a team working together to create a future with the Verein. As mentioned before, this includes creating training possibilities, subsidizing workshops and providing a platform for people to learn and share.

With the 100 CHF fee per year we pay the rent for our training space, we invite teachers to hold workshops, and we support acro-related events and projects.
